What we do

Common jobs include repairs, upgrades, and small builds. If your project is not listed, send a message anyway. If it is in my wheelhouse, I will tell you. If it is not, I will tell you that too.

Drywall and texture

Repairs, patching, seam fixes, texture blending, and clean prep for paint.

Painting

Interior and exterior painting with real prep work, clean lines, and even coverage.

Framing and general repairs

Small framing, trim fixes, reinforcement, and the kind of repairs that make a home feel solid again.

Deck repairs and refresh

Replace damaged boards, tighten up loose sections, correct problem areas, and restore a clean finished look.

Entryways and steps

Fix wobbly steps, repair landings, improve safety, and clean up the look so your entry does not scream for help.

Railing repair and install

Secure loose rails, repair sections, and install sturdy handrails for safer decks, stairs, and walkways.

Structural support and framing

Reinforce supports, repair posts, and correct problem spots that cause bounce, movement, or long term damage.

Prep work and cleanup

Scraping, removal, and surface prep that sets the stage for a clean finish.
